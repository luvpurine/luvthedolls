# luvthedolls


## An informational website appreciating the efforts and lives of transfeminine individuals.

luvthedolls is a website that focuses on bringing visibility and compassion to those who identify as transfeminine (which is a term for feminine-leaning individuals under the transgender umbrella). Even in the contemporary era, transfeminine people are denied opportunities, abandoned by their loved ones, and even brutally murdered for their identities. I hope that my website will help those in and out of this community to learn more about this identity, ways to help such injustices, and honor the lives of transfeminine dolls.

In this website, a user can:
* learn about transfeminine as a terminology
* find resources about gender dysphoria, HRT, ways to help, and more
* look at some inspirational dolls in different fields
* appreciate those whose lives have been lost
